The missed-call AI system every mechanic should have

Read time: 3 minutes

A mechanic's phone rings while they're under a car, on another call, or closing up. The call goes to voicemail — and most callers don't leave one. They just dial the next shop. Every missed call is a customer who was ready to spend money, gone in the time it takes to wipe your hands.

💡 AI Money Move

Install a missed-call text back. The instant a call is missed, AI automatically texts the caller:

"Sorry we missed your call — this is [Shop]. We're with a customer but we can help. What are you bringing in, and when works for you?"

The caller replies by text instead of calling a competitor. You answer when you have a free minute, and the lead is still warm.

📊 Local Pro Case Study

A two-bay auto repair shop was missing a lot of calls during busy hours.

  • Before: ~40 missed calls per month and no system to catch them. Most never called back.
  • After: Every missed call triggered an instant text. A large share of those callers replied, and the shop booked extra jobs each month from calls that used to vanish.

The phone was already ringing. They just stopped letting the money hang up.

🛠️ Tool of the Week

A texting platform connected to your business line that detects missed calls and auto-sends a text. Pair it with your booking calendar so the conversation can go straight to a scheduled appointment.

✍️ Script of the Week

Sorry we missed you! This is [Shop]. Reply here and we'll get you sorted — what's going on with the vehicle, and is today or tomorrow better for a drop-off?

It acknowledges the miss, stays friendly, and moves toward a booking.

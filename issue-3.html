Realtors: use AI to revive old buyer and seller leads

Read time: 3 minutes

Every realtor is sitting on a goldmine they've forgotten about: the contacts in their CRM from 6, 12, or 24 months ago. People who inquired, got busy, and dropped off. They didn't all buy elsewhere — many just never heard from anyone again. Chasing brand-new leads is expensive. Waking up old ones is nearly free.

💡 AI Money Move

Run an AI old-lead reactivation campaign. AI works through your dead database and sends a low-pressure, personal-feeling message to each contact:

"Hi [Name], it's [Agent] — we connected a while back about [buying/selling]. The market's shifted since then. Are you still thinking about a move this year, or is that on hold for now?"

The "still thinking or on hold?" framing is easy to answer and surfaces the people who are quietly ready to go.

📊 Local Pro Case Study

A solo agent had hundreds of old buyer and seller leads sitting untouched in a spreadsheet.

  • Before: ~300 cold contacts, zero follow-up, written off as dead.
  • After: An AI win-back sequence messaged the list over a few days. A meaningful slice replied, several booked calls, and the campaign produced new active clients from contacts that had cost nothing extra to acquire.

The leads were already paid for. They just needed someone to reach out.

🛠️ Tool of the Week

A CRM with bulk SMS/email automation plus simple list segmentation, so you can separate buyers from sellers and tailor the win-back message to each.

✍️ Script of the Week

Hey [Name], just checking in — are you still looking for help with [buying/selling], or has the timing changed? Either way is totally fine, just want to point you in the right direction.

Reusable for almost any service business, not just real estate.
